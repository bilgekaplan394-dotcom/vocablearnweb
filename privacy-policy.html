Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

VocabLearn! is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data on our servers.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how VocabLearn! ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our Android application. By using VocabLearn!, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

VocabLearn! itself does not collect any personal information. All data you create — word lists, study progress, streaks, and statistics — is stored locally on your device only.

However, our app uses third-party services that may collect certain data:

  • Google AdMob — used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services — required for app distribution and updates.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Word lists and translations you add
  • Study progress and completed phases
  • Streak count and study statistics
  • App settings and preferences
  • Selected native language

4. Word Packages

When you download a word package, the app fetches JSON files from our public GitHub repository. No personal data is sent during this process — only a standard HTTP request is made to retrieve the word list file.

5. Advertising

VocabLearn! displays interstitial ads provided by Google AdMob after completing study phases. These ads may be personalized based on your interests using the Advertising ID on your device.

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time through your device settings:

  • Go to Settings → Google → Ads
  • Enable Opt out of Ads Personalization

6. Permissions

VocabLearn! requests the following permissions:

  • INTERNET — to download word packages and display ads
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to send daily study reminders (you can disable this)
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to reschedule daily reminders after device restart
  • AD_ID — required by Google AdMob for advertising

7. Children's Privacy

VocabLearn! is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security settings. We recommend using a screen lock and keeping your device's operating system up to date.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no account to delete or data to request. You can delete all app data at any time through:

  • Settings → Apps → VocabLearn! → Storage → Clear Data

This permanently removes all locally stored words, progress, and settings from your device.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: contact@vocablearn.app

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
